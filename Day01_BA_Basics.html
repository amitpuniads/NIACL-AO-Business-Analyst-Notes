üîÑ Reinsurance

Reinsurance (‡§™‡•Å‡§®‡§∞‡•ç‡§¨‡•Ä‡§Æ‡§æ) is "insurance for insurance companies." Insurers transfer portions of their risk to reinsurers to reduce exposure to large claims.

Why Reinsurance?
  • Risk distribution across multiple parties
  • Protection against catastrophic losses
  • Improved solvency and financial stability
  • Increased underwriting capacity

About NIACL

New India Assurance Company Limited (NIACL) is India's largest general insurance company, established in 1919. It's a government-owned company under the Ministry of Finance.

Key Facts:
  • Headquarters: Mumbai, Maharashtra
  • Founded: 1919
  • Type: Public Sector General Insurance Company
  • Business Lines: Motor, Health, Fire, Marine, Engineering, Crop, Aviation, etc.
  • Network: 2000+ offices across India and international presence
